Why do you ride bikes?

Riding bikes is an experience everyone can enjoy. Old and young, big and small, any ethinicity, nationality can commune with nature and other humans in exactly the same way. Pushing each other to get stronger, braver, skinnier and in turn enjoying the bike even more. In a technologically advanced and sometimes toxic culture all one has to do to escape back to the root of whats good about being human. Strap on a helmet, through a leg over the bike and start pedaling. Adaptations begin immediately and so do the grins. This website is all about documenting experiences on the bike and diplaying art influenzed by riding them.
